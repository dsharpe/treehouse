About us

Treehouse is a small software consulting firm with big ambitions. Our consultants can help with all aspects of project delivery from designing the solution to seeing the implementation through to timely completion.

Large firms have complex business objectives - at Treehouse Technology our focus is enabling those objectives with software.

Our mission

At Treehouse Technology Corp it is our mission to help you achieve your loftiest goals - assuming your goals involve building and integrating enterprise software systems.

Talk to us about your goals so we can help you get there.

Success stories

U.S. Department of Veterans Affairs

CRM Enterprise Self-service Platform

Led the design and development of a complete multi-tier enterprise self-service platform which includes a web portal for benefits management, a turbotax-like platform for electronic form submission, and a complete system integration platform consisting of over 200 integrations with about 40 partner systems.

Under our leadership, the project grew to a team of over 100 people. We are proud to have played a major part in every management and technical decision made on a such a successful program.

Affinion Loyalty Group

CRM Loyalty Implementation / PRM Integration Platform

Successfully launched several websites for General Motors' dealer reimbursed marketing programs. The innovative, dynamic system architecture enables the rapid implementation of new programs as well as easy expansion of existing ones.

CareCloud

Medical Practice Management and Revenue Cycle Management

Led the delivery of a complete enterprise software platform.
Vetted and chose the technologies, hired and structured the technical teams, and created the platform architecture and system design.

Features include fully-scalable SOA-based PAAS, integrated document management and retrieval system (Alfresco), integrated workflow engine (Intalio), LDAP/SSO security layer, enterprise portal, and enterprise reports engine.

The result is a revolutionary, multiple award-winning medical practice management solution. Awards include IBM SmartCamp Silicon Valley 2010 (1st place), Winner of The Beacon Award in Information Technology category, Red Herring Top 100 Tech Startup, among others.

Dominion Resources

Billing System Integration and Partner Integration

Designed and developed Dominion Power's electronic billing website complete with real-time integration with legacy mainframe billing system, a third party vendor for electronic payments, and monitoring software.

Interval International

Legacy System Conversion and Integration

Consulted on defining processes and program structure to enable a 3-year, $38M re-architecture of in-house systems from AS/400 to SOA-based JEE applications. Led architecture, design, and project management discussions to guide the client toward an overall system design and phasing approach that would enable their short- and long-term goals.
